Admission

Freshman must/be/submit:

  • High School graduate(Form 138)
  • National Career Assessment Examination(NCAE) result
  • Pass the NEU Entrance Examination
  • Pass the NEU English Proficiency Test
  • Certification of Good Moral Character from the last school attended
  • Physically and mentaly fit
  • Two pieces 2x2 latest colored picture
  • R201 file for Iglesia Ni Cristo members
  • Endorsement by an Iglesia Ni Cristo member/pastor, for non-member applicant

    • Retention Policy

    A student is expected to demenstrate good scholastic performance and must maintain passing grade in every course to be retained in the program. University rules on pre-requisite and co-requisite apply.
